About

A sailing voyage needs some planning even for an experienced sailor. Voyager is a checklist for Sailors to pack & plan before they set off on an overnight voyage.

We have been apart of planning voyages for sailors from departure points including Cape Town, Sydney and Dublin. Looking forward to be part of your voyage.

Checklist

Let’s get you started
There are a lot of complex things in the world, this doesn’t have to be one of them. Let’s break it down for you.
  1. Select the item you have packed or prepared from the pre-populated list or add your own item by typing in your item by clicking/tapping on the “+” button.
  2. Select “To buy” option next to each item if you still need to buy it. Add the quantity & estimated cost. Then select “Calculate” to see total items to buy and the estimated cost. If you enter you email address, you can email the list.
  3. Find an online or physical sailing store to purchase what you need.
  4. Add a review to give us feedback on how you use Voyager.
